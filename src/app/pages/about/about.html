About Education House

Guiding Students Toward Global Success Since 2011

Education House is a full-service education consultancy helping students prepare for international exams, choose the right university, and confidently navigate the visa process — all under one roof.

🎯

Our Mission

To empower every student with the knowledge, skills and confidence needed to pursue quality education anywhere in the world — through honest guidance, expert coaching and unwavering support.

🔭

Our Vision

To become India's most trusted name in education consultancy, recognised for transforming ambition into achievement for over a million students by 2035.

Director's Message

"Every student who walks through our doors deserves a plan built around their dreams."

When we started Education House in 2011, our goal was simple: give students honest advice instead of sales pitches. Fifteen years later, that principle still guides everything we do — from how we train our counsellors to how we design our courses.

I'm proud that thousands of our students are now studying, working and building lives across six countries. But what I'm proudest of is that they got there on a plan that was right for them, not just what was easiest for us to sell.

Anjali Kapoor
Founder & Director, Education House
