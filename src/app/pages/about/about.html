About Education House

Nurturing Young Minds in Lucknow

Education House is a coaching institute dedicated to students from Class 1 to Class 8 — helping every child build a strong academic foundation while growing into a confident, disciplined and curious individual.

🎯

Our Mission

To nurture curious, confident and capable young learners by providing quality education in a caring, supportive and engaging environment where every child receives personal attention and opportunities to grow academically and personally.
